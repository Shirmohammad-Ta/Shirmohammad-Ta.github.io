Shirmohammad Tavangari | Stanford Medicine

I am an Artificial Intelligence Researcher at Stanford University School of Medicine, collaborating with Prof. Jayakumar Rajadas. I also work with Prof. Isabeau Prémont-Schwarz at Mila and serve as Chief AI Scientist at NellaDerm Inc. leading AI initiatives funded by a Google grant.

My research spans Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Applied Mathematics. I am honored as a TEDxSanabad Speaker, recipient of the Young Scientist Award 2022, and Editor of AI-focused volumes published by Springer Nature. Currently, I serve as Associate Editor for Springer Applied Intelligence.
